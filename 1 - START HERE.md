# Solnest Cinematic Director Setup Kit - Start Here

Welcome. This kit gets the Solnest Cinematic Director, your cinematic listing-video director, running inside your Claude Desktop app. You paste a listing link, walk away, and it hands you back a finished 20 to 25 second luxury walkthrough video, ready to post.

You will not touch any code. Claude reads this kit and walks you through every single step, and it checks each one actually works before moving on. Plan on about 15 to 20 minutes, one time only. After that, making a video takes one sentence.

---

## Have these ready before you start

Tick these off first. Most of the frustration the first time around came from missing one of these, so do not skip this list.

- [ ] **Claude Desktop app**, signed in on the **Pro or Max plan.** This matters: the Solnest Cinematic Director connects three tools, and the free Claude plan only allows one connector. Pro or Max is required. There is no way around this on the free plan.
- [ ] **Higgsfield account** with some credits on it. This is the tool that generates the video clips. A small credit pack is plenty for several videos.
- [ ] **Firecrawl account.** The free plan is fine. You will need its API key, which starts with `fc-`. This is the tool that reads the listing photos. (The guide shows you exactly where to find the key.)
- [ ] **Descript account** on the **Hobbyist plan or higher.** This is the tool that stitches the clips into one video with music. The free Descript plan stamps a watermark on your video and caps the quality, so Hobbyist or higher is what you want for clean, postable output.
- [ ] **Google account.** Only needed if you want finished videos dropped straight into Google Drive. This one is optional.

If you already connected Higgsfield or Firecrawl in the past, great. Claude will check what is already working and skip those.

---

## How to run it (this is the whole thing)

1. Open the **Claude Desktop app.**
2. Start a **new chat.**
3. **Drag this entire folder** into the message box. If your computer will not let you drag a whole folder, open the folder, select all the files inside it, and drag those in instead.
4. Type this and send it:

   > Set me up with the Solnest Cinematic Director. Follow the SETUP GUIDE in this kit and walk me through it one step at a time.

5. Follow along. Claude does the rest, one step at a time, and confirms each piece before continuing.

That is the entire job. When you finish, you will paste any Airbnb, VRBO, or Zillow link, say "make me a Solnest video," and it runs the whole pipeline for you.

You will not break anything. If a step does not look like the guide says it should, just tell Claude what you are seeing on your screen, in plain words, and it will help you sort it out. There is also a full **TROUBLESHOOTING** sheet in the `reference` folder that covers the common snags and the exact fix for each one.
